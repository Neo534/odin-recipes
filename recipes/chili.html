Chili

A wonderful chili made by world renown chef.

The recipe hails from the steep hills of Argonne, France where chef Micheal Puotie crafted the dish from fresh, wild herbs. It can be found in his restaurant Taste of France.

This spicy yet flavorfull rendition will have you coming back for more while your touring the region. Many a tourist has made a second stop at the world famous restaurant before catching a flight home.

Indgredients

  • onions
  • bell pepper
  • diced tomatoes
  • tomato paste
  • chili powder
  • cumin
  • beef
  • turkey stock

Steps

  1. saute onion and pepper
  2. add beef and cook
  3. add tomatoes and paste
  4. add tureky stock
  5. add spice
  6. simmer for one hour
