Pancakes

This beautiful cheesecake recipe was made by Italian master chef Greg Habbot

Ever wake up on a Sunday morning and wish you could have hot, fluffy restaurant quality pancakes in your own home? This recipe will show you how.

A simple yet elegant recipe for the novice home cook to cut his teeth. There is plenty of room for error if it doesn't turn out perfect, and if you accidentally burn them you can always feed them to the dog.

Ingredients

  • baking powder
  • egg
  • milk
  • flour
  • salt
  • sugar
  • nutmeg
  • oil

Steps

  1. mix milk, egg, vanilla extract and oil
  2. separately mix rest of ingredients
  3. combine the two
  4. in a preheated skillet pour a small amount of batter
  5. flip when bubbles appear in center
